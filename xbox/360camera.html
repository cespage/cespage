Features

Quality  - High-end online camera supports up to VGA 640x480 video at 30 frames per second and takes 1.3-megapixel still photos.

Messenging  - The camera includes a microphone that connects to the controller, so everyone in the room can chat online with their friends. The camera supports built-in video chat and video messaging on Xbox Live.

Personalise  - Your face can be imported into games and can also be placed next to your name on leader boards for even more recognition or fame.

Xbox Live  - Chat with your friends while you play online in Xbox 360 games as well as in the Xbox Live Arcade.
