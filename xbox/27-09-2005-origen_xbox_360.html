Origen Xbox 360 Website Mystery Revealed

For over five weeks this mysterious website has been counting down, but counting down to what - today the countdown is over and all has been revealed!

By CESPage.com Xbox, Published 27th September 2005

The Countdown is over!

After many weeks of counting down, it has finally been revealed today what the countdown was for.

Origen is the place for European gamers to play on the Xbox 360 before anyone else, we may not have Every10Minutes or similar competition, but this is the next best thing!
Origen is a real place, it is a chance to play on the next generation Xbox before anyone else, where you can play on all the Xbox launch titles and take on other gamers. Its time to apply now go to origenxbox360.com to get your chance to play games and with other gamers on the Xbox 360.

This is a competition open to anyone over 18 living in UK, Ireland, France, Germany, Spain, Italy, the Netherlands, Denmark, Finland, Norway, Sweden, Belgium, Switzerland, Austria, Portugal or Greece. Click on the Golden Fruit and then answer a few questions about the Xbox 360 and say the Xbox 360 is the best thing since... in 100 characters, the funniest entry stands a chance for one of two invites to this exclusive event.

For the past few weeks CESPage.com Xbox has been following the countdown and changes on origenxbox360.com with the bonsai tree with green apples on it and the green rabbits, it was a mystery until now what it all meant, what did the apples represent, what were the rabbits for.
Also many hints appeared on the internet plus in a couple of newsletters from Microsoft Xbox in Europe featured an image of two green rabbits that linked to the website. Many speculated what it would be, such as the release dates and pricing - it was not this as this was announced well before today.
Was it announcement of Halo 3 information or the launch titles, all was merely speculation, but now with the end of the countdown, all has been revealed.
