CESPage.com Xbox Weekly Update Four

Launch dates for Xbox 360 console release, Bizarre Creations announcement, Origen Xbox 360 website, and more

By CESPage.com Xbox, Published 18th September 2005

Xbox 360 Launch Announcement

The website Origen Xbox 360 is not counting down to the announcement of the release date as this was announced at the Tokyo Game Show this week. The release date for North America is 22nd November, Europe is 2nd December and Japan is 10th of December. Here in the weekly update we will indicate how many days to go this will be, starting from this week.

65 Days to North American Release of Xbox 360
75 Days to Europe Release of Xbox 360
83 Days to Japanese Release of Xbox 360

Bizarre Creations Countdown

Well at least one mystery has been solved the countdown that appeared on the bizarreonline.net website has completed. However it was counting down to a press release, which was leaked the previous day, so appeared on the website early.
The announcement was a partnership to create a next-generation franchise with SEGA, with SEGA marketing and distributing the as unannounced title from Bizarre Creations.
This title will most likely be as hinted by the press release based on existing SEGA IP or ideas, hopefully it will not be long before this title is actually announced.

Source : Bizarre Creations

Origen Xbox 360 Week 5

Just over a week to go on this mysterious website yet another update has occurred two more apples have appeared on the tree, and the rabbits appear on either side of the bonsai tree.
What is the website counting down too, it could be to the updating of the Xbox.com website to a brand new layout, an update to the forums there is anticipated and the date this is due to occur has not been announced so this may be what it is for.

View : www.origenxbox360.com

Xbox 360 Launch Dates Update

If you look back at weekly update two from the 4th of September you will notice some speculation about the launch date, this of course has been announced and one of the dates was actually correct, although for the wrong region, a staggered release schedule is indeed what has been announced.
Will CESPage.com Xbox be correct about the true purpose of the Origen Xbox 360 website being a promotion site for a new look Xbox.com or maybe just a release of marketing materials for the new console, whatever it turns out to be you will find out here.
