Features

Storyline  - The Master Chief after his exploits in the game of the year Halo : Combat Evolved must now battle the alien Covenant on Earth and prevent the destruction of all mankind.

Gameplay  - Improved graphics, enhanced AI, destructible vehicles, interactive environments and more.

Weapons & Vehicles  - New weapons including duel wielding and play as then Covenant Elite and hold the energy sword for yourself with it's awesome melee attack. New vehicles including multiplayer air combat and vehicle boarding plus new variants of the old vehicles including a rocket launching Warthog and speed-boosted Ghost.

Multiplayer  - Expanding on the excelent multiplayer features of the original Halo this is even better including Xbox Live online play including clans and customisable armour items and online stats.
