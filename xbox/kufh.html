Everyone imagines oneself being in the center of it all, as the hero who decides the outcome of the battle. With Kingdom Under Fire: Heroes, players will have a chance to do exactly that: commanding hundreds of warriors to fierce battles, slaying enemies with your own blade, and accumulating a military force mighty enough to save the world from a catastrophic disaster.

Features

Gameplay  - Once you choose the main character, the war campaigns start out with one infantry unit. You will have to quickly learn how to move the soldiers to advantageous battlegrounds, how to counter enemy attacks, how to dispose enemy captains for a landslide victory, how to operate with multiple units of different class simultaneously, etc, until you become the ultimate warlord.

Combat  - Slay enemies with stylish combos, special skills, and magic spells. Command hundreds of soldiers with your own strategy to the ultimate victory. Over 35 different types of troops with diverse abilities. (Soldiers, Beasts, Dragons, Catapults, etc.)

Environment  - 200 soldiers fighting to death, earth shattering Stone Golems, catapults launching huge rocks, celestial bodies summoned to crush enemies... The action is more explosive than ever, yet the strategy even deeper. The Single Player Mode is over 50 hours of epic fantasy warfare breathtakingly rendered with cutting edge 3D graphics.

Xbox Live  Fully supporting the latest Live 3.0 features in 3 different Multiplayer Modes, you can defend a fortress with your allies, destroy your opponents' armies, or simply fight out single-handedly to find out who is the strongest warrior of all. Replay Save, Observer Mode, and Fair-match Options will help you pick up the necessary skills to enjoy the total war experience.
