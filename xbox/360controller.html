Based on the acclaimed design of the Xbox Controller S, the Xbox 360 Controller features an extended three metre cable and an improved ergonomic design. The centralised, glowing Xbox Guide Button grants you quick access to your digital movie, music, and games libraries. The ultimate in gamer flexibility, precision, control, quality, and comfort are all yours.

Features

Design  - New and improved ergonomic design enables maximum comfort and endless gameplay. A port for your Xbox 360 Headset is included.

Precision  - Six analogue buttons with 256 levels of sensitivity. Two analogue pressure-point triggers, two vibration feedback motors, and one eight-way directional pad. New left and right shoulder buttons are designed for ease of use.

Personalise  - Set the Rumble Control to Full, Medium, Low, or Off to match your personal preference.

Xbox Guide  - Xbox Guide Button at the centre of the controller allows quick access to all the main Xbox 360 system activities, such as watching movies, listening to music, contacting friends on Xbox Live, and customising your gaming experiences. Easily jump to the Xbox Gamer Guide or Xbox Live Marketplace.
