Experience the Mortal Kombat franchise from an entirely new first and/or third person action/adventure perspective. For the first time ever, explore all of the mythos and rumours of the Mortal Kombat universe starring two of the all-time Mortal Kombat favourites, Shaolin Monks: Liu Kang and Kung Lao.

Features

Storyline  - See the infamous Mortal Kombat Tournament from the eyes of Earthrealm's mightiest combatant Shaolin Monks Liu Kang and Kung Lao. Explore the Outworld in its entirety...revisit the classic Mortal Kombat 1 and 2 stages in 3D! Fight and interact with a slew of classic Mortal Kombat characters, such as Sub-Zero, Scorpion, Kitana and Goro.

Attack  - First Ever MK Action/Adventure with Multi-Directional Kombat System, Levy attacks in any direction at any time, all the while not losing focus on the primary target. Experience incredible depth of combat via Strings, Juggles, Special Attacks, Air Combat and Fatalities.

Characters  - Choose from several paths in which to spend experience points gained from battle! Augment your hero's abilities for incredible feats of agility, such as running along walls...or choose to augment any of 4 trees of Special Attacks to unlock new attacks unique for each character.

Environment  - Melt enemies in acid pits, impale them on spiked ceilings, and hang them from hooks. Combine background interactions with Combat to solve unique action-orientated puzzles. Hurl an enemy into a spiked wall and leap from their impaled body to a higher area!
