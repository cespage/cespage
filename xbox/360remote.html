The Xbox 360 Universal Media Remote was engineered to serve as your integrated control centre for the entire Xbox 360 experience. Enter a new world of digital entertainment with the touch of a single button. Play DVD movies and music, plus control your TV and your Media Center PC with one controller.

Features

Console  - Power your Xbox 360 system on or off without leaving the couch. The Xbox Guide Button allows for easy navigation of the Xbox Gamer Guide. A, B, X, and Y buttons are included for basic control of your Xbox 360 system, even without a controller.

Control  - The Xbox 360 Universal Media Remote includes control buttons for your music, pictures, and more. The remote supports universal TV and console functionality, including volume and mute buttons and up-and-down channel changing.

DVD  - The Xbox 360 supports progressive-scan for DVD playback functionality, including a control for opening and closing the tray.

Media Center  - Windows XP Media Center Edition Button and Media Center Edition functionality are featured.
