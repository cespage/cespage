At the core of Saint's Row is urban culture. However, rather than being a serious gang simulator, Saint's Row takes its look at gangs through the lens of hip-hop and rap videos. The end result is an exciting heightened world of big rims, expensive clothes, and gold plated guns.

Features

No Load Time and No Limits  - Saint's Row is designed with freedom in mind. The unique, open-world environment offers a non linear game experience transporting the player into a living, breathing world which is unlike anything else they've encountered. The whole world of Saint's Row is open to exploration from the very start of the game and all parts of Stilwater's exteriors and interiors are instantaneously streamed, meaning the player will not experience any frustrating loading time.

Image  - Fully dynamically-lit world, next gen shaders, specularity mapping, reflection mapping, and a world built for High Definition, combine to create effects previous reserved for movies. Rag doll physics, extensive car damage, realistic pedestrian and traffic AI, all make Saint's Row one of the richest gaming environments around.

Character  - Tired of being told who to be? With thousands of character creation options available, the player can literally create anyone, even themselves. Over a hundred different clothing types allow the player to customize his look. Throw in an extensive car modification system and Saint's Row is poised to set the bar in character customisation.

Xbox Live  - What's the fun of doing a drive-by if you can't have three of your buddies shootin' with you? For the first time in an open-world, city based game, you can now take your skills online. Players can form gangs, recruit other players, and compete for cash and respect in many different online modes.
