Xbox 360 Launch Date

While this has not yet been announced there is speculation about when the Xbox 360 will be released, while the cost is known this is not. One date is Friday the 25th of November however this is around the American Thanksgiving holiday which Thursday the 24th. However CESPage.com Xbox is going for 14th of November or 18th of November which are a Monday and Friday respectively. The 14th may be a familiar date to existing Xbox owners as it is the date shown when the Xbox is first turned on (though the year is 2001). The Xbox 360 will be released around the same time but a slightly staggered release is not unlikely for example it could be North America and Canada launch on 14th/ 18th November, Europe (including United Kingdom) on 25th November, for Japan it may be possibly 2nd December, on of the previous dates or later than both.
