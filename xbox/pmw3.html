Features

Storyline  After mysteriously having been transported out of Pac-Village and into this strange and different land, it is up to Pac-Man to uncover Erwin's plot and save the world. Along the way, Pac-Man will meet a host of unexpected "old friends," and in order to defeat the ultimate enemy, he might just have to turn his old foes into allies!

New Moves  - Now you can climb fences, jump up walls, swing around poles and punch enemies! Special feature now allows players to control Pinky and Clyde.

Power-ups  - Store an arsenal of power-ups like the Power Ribbon, Time Decelerator, Electro-Charge, Chrome Power, and Super Butt Bounce...and super-charge them by collecting special fruits!

Pac-Man  - Get transported into classic 2D and 3D Pac-Man mazes to compete for bonuses. Includes the original Pac-Man arcade game from 1980!
