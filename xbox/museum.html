Single largest compilation of Namco Arcade Classics ever to grace console gaming systems. The new retro menu interface allows players to explore a virtual arcade hall where each of the classic games will be standing in its original arcade form...except this time no coins will be required!

Features

Pac Man  Play the most popular arcade game of all time! Navigate the yellow fellow through the original Pac-Man maze, avoid ghosts while chomping pellets and fruit, and use power pellets to turn the ghosts blue and get some payback! Also there is Ms Pac Man Starring Pac-Man's female counterpart; explore, collect, and chomp your way through four different mazes and eat new fruits.

Galaga & Galaxian  - Pilot a space ship, fend off frantic swarms of bee-like aliens, try your luck with "Challenging Stages," and find the best way to deal with enemies' powerful tractor beams!. Also Galaxian which is a precursor to Galaga, destroy flying aliens as they move in from formation to attack you.

Pole Position  - A milestone in racing games, players drive fast to beat the timer, and avoid cars and hazards or risk exploding. There is also the qequel to Pole Position, this title introduced four new race tracks - Fuji, Test, Suzuka, and Seaside.

Xbox Live  - Players will also be able to post high scores to the Xbox Live Leaderboards
