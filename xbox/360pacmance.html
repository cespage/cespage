Created in honour of the first-ever Xbox 360 Pac-Man World Championship, the Pac-Man Championship Edition brings you back to the days of pizza parlours and arcades of the '80s while raising the bar with the first brand new mazes in more than 26 years, available exclusively on Xbox LIVE Arcade on the Xbox 360.

Publisher : Microsoft Xbox Live Arcade

Release Date : Available Now
Price: 400 Points

Features

Gameplay  - Classic 2D Pac-Man presentation with a new modern look and in high-definition with Championship Mode created especially for the finals competition at the Xbox 360 Pac-Man World Championship by NAMCO BANDAI Games and Toru Iwatani, the original creator of Pac-Man.

Challenge Mode  - This mode is an evolution of the Championship Mode featuring large changes in the Pac-Dot arrangement and a new colour scheme. During game play, Patience mazes strategically offer fewer Power Pellets and Reward mazes provide a large number of Power Pellets - providing unique challenges and rewards for the player.

Xbox Live  - Compare your performance against other "Pac-Man" fans from around the world on Xbox LIVE Leaderboards and Earn up to 12 Achievements and 200 Gamerscore.
