Play as Shadow the Hedgehog, a rebel haunted by his dark and mysterious past who must fight alone to discover his true identity. His only hope is to collect the seven Chaos Emeralds in order to uncover the secrets of his past and the shape of his future. The planet is under attack from a fierce alien force that threatens the future of Westopolis and government troops are battling anyone they can't trust. With Dr. Eggman after the emeralds for his own evil means and Sonic and the gang playing their part, the fight is on and time is running out for Shadow to decide which side he will choose.

Features

Destiny  - Take full control of Shadow's destiny - you choose the path that he will take to help him discover the truth about his dark and mysterious past.

Powers  - Master Shadow's unique Special Powers including 'Chaos Control' manipulating both time and space and 'Chaos Blast' for ultimate destruction.

Weapons  - Choose to use a variety of weapons and vehicles from both sides including alien craft, government vehicles and laser guns.

Experience  - With branching paths and multiple endings it's a new experience every time you play!
