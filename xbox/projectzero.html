Project Zero will chill you to the bone as you become immersed in a world full of supernatural spirits and sheer terror. Paranoia is just the beginning in this psycho thriller. As the mystery unravels so will your nerves as Project Zero takes you farther and farther into the realm of the paranormal, where what you can't see is what terrorises you the most.

Features

Ghosts  - Face up to your fears with each gruesome apparition having it's own personality, whether it's a hostile enemy ghost, just wandering through this world or only visible through Miku's camera.

Camera  - Obtain spirits power in Miku's camera and unlock special abilities such as Telephoto, Auto-Search and the power to make ghosts' movements slower.

Audio  - Experience the thrills of surround sound with Dolby 5.1 channel compatibility. The enhanced audio will make the feeling of being attacked a terrifyingly real experience.

Environment  - Eerie environmental sounds, fleeting spirits, and a terror-filled story line all add up to one of the scariest games on the Xbox.
