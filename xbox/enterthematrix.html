Enter the Matrix is the game from the world of the Matrix featuring original action and storyline that both complement and intersect the elaborate Matrix story line, Enter the Matrix provides immersive, movie-like settings and even see remnants of the gameplay surface in the film, The Matrix Reloaded and lead to the events in The Matrix Revolutions!

Features

Gameplay  - Experience action and exploration to fighting and driving within the world that is The Matrix and beyond.

Fighting & Weapons  - Face up to the Agents with weapons such as guns, fists, and your wits. Also master the art of Kung Fu and re-write the laws of physics with Bullet Time!

Environment  - Survive car chases, stunts, and shootouts also see many of the environments from The Matrix Reloaded such as the Chateau, Freeway and other parts of the City and more.

Movie  - Interact with likenesses and voiceovers from key characters from The Matrix Reloaded: Including Neo, Trinity, Morpheus, Niobe, and Ghost. Watch nearly one full hour of exclusive movie footage, starring The Matrix film actors such as Trinity, The Oracle and others.
