General

Xbox 360 delivers the most powerful console, the best games, the next generation of Xbox Live, and unique digital entertainment experiences that all revolve around you.

The Most Powerful Console

Experience Superior Game Play

  • Symmetrical triple-core CPU, each core running at 3.2GHz
  • 512MB of unified system memory
  • Custom 500MHz ATI graphics processor made for HD graphics
  • All Xbox 360 games are optimised for Hi Definition and Standard TVs with 730p/1080i output and 16x9 cinematic aspect ratio
  • Anti-aliasing for smooth textures - no more jaggies
  • Full surround sound
  • Built-in wireless receiver for precision game play

The Power of Choice

  • Choose from two console to fit your gaming needs. The fully-loaded Xbox 360 System of the basic Xbox 360 Core System.
  • The sleek Xbox 360 design works in either the vertical or horizontal position - you choose
  • Family Settings; Family-friendly entertainment through customised settings

The Best Games

The Greatest Games Portfolio Ever

  • All Xbox 360 games are in HD and Xbox Live-enabled
  • The biggest names in publishing are making exclusive Xbox 360 games available at launch
  • Multi-platform games will look and play best on Xbox 360
  • Backwards Compatible, top-selling Xbox games can be played on Xbox 360, including Halo 2
  • Achievements; Show off and keep track of your performance and progression
  • User-created content. Customisation features let you design the game you want to play
  • 25-40 titles shipping in the launch window - the strongest launch lineup ever
  • Over 160 games already in development today

The Next Generation of Xbox Live

Xbox Live an Xbox 360 is the best way to play with friends and family and get the most out of your gaming experience

Competition

  • Xbox Live Gold is the best online gaming experience with intelligent matchmaking, leagues, ladders, tournaments, and more
  • Challenge people from around the block or around the world
  • Spectator mode; Watch and cheer on other gamers while they compete

Community

  • Xbox Live Silver: Instantly become a member of the always-on Xbox Live community at no extra charge when Xbox 360 is connected to broadband
  • Live Aware: Xbox 360 keeps you connect to your friends list
  • Digital Identity: Establish yourself as a unique contributor to the 2 million plus member Xbox Live community with your gamertag, gamer picture, gamer card, reputation, and friends list.
  • Communicate: Talk to friends and competitors in or out of game with voice chat, text messaging, message centre, and video messaging (coming soon)

No Limits

  • One unified service: Talk and interact whether you are on Xbox 360 or Xbox
  • Marketplace: Try out new games with free demos and expand your existing games with free and premium content
  • Programming: Gain access to the Xbox Live Calendar as well as online gaming events that are regularly updated and evolving.
  • Online Games: Every Xbox 360 game is Xbox Live online enabled

Xbox 360 is the ultimate digital amplifier for music, movies, photos and more

Play all Types of Media

  • Play DVDs in progressive scan CDs right out of the box

  • Xbox Guide Button: Access your music and movies with the touch of a button; even turn your Xbox 360 system on and off from the comfort of your couch

Share and Customise

  • Create Custom play lists and soundtracks for games
  • Build photo slideshows and add music to display on the living room television

  • 3-Month Value Pack : 3-Month subscription plus headset and additional features
