Features

Gameplay  - Bring down the house with the only console Karaoke engine that lets players sing to nearly any song; digitally enhance favorite music with various audio features; and create custom photo slide shows enhanced with unique soundtracks.

Karaoke  - Thanks to the included microphone, players can showcase their vocal talents or lack thereof, by singing along to more than a dozen of the most requested Karaoke songs.

Media Player  - Bring the club experience home with the interactive media player, allowing anyone to mix, match, shuffle, and customise soundtracks. Players can now enhance the experience with the addition of interactive 2D and 3D on-screen visualizers.

Xbox Live  - Take the multimedia experience from the den to the living room. With PC media sharing, Xbox players can easily transfer digital music and digital photos to their Xbox, creating the ultimate music and video library. And if that's not enough, feel free to download new music, visualisers and Karaoke songs via Xbox Live.
