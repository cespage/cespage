Features

Gameplay  Stunning visual effects and cinematic atmosphere, deadly new stealth tactics, cutting-edge espionage weapons and equipment, play as spies or mercenaries in a revolutionary, online multiplayer mode.

Character  - Sam Fisher has been on the front lines of espionage throughout the past decades. Has excelled in the field of covert operations through work, curiosity, and honesty. Has little time for niceties and even less for lies.

Environments  - Immerse yourself with revolutionary lighting, shadowing, animation, physics, and sound effects are pushed to create a universe where the line between reality and gaming starts to blur.

Xbox Live  - From protecting the Federal Bank against terrorist threat to securing the River Mall from a rebel strike. It's two-on-two tactical espionage with two Xbox-exclusive multiplayer maps, premium content you can download from Xbox Live.
