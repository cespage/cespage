The game tells the story of Riddick's dramatic escape from the previously in-escapable triple-max security prison Butcher Bay, home to the most violent prisoners in the galaxy. The events in the game preceed those in the hit movie Pitch Black starring Vin Diesel.

Features

Gameplay  - Reveal the secrets of Riddick's past and experience the game with Vin Diesel likeness plus voiceover in the English language version.

Fighting  - Frenetic first-person shooter action, stealth, and brutal hand-to-hand combat and also use knives and fists, or sneak up on enemies and take them down.

Stealth  - Seamless transition to stealth moves and tactics for a deeper, more cohesive adventure. Also included is Riddick's trademark "eye-shine" enabling you to see in the dark and provides a whole new element of stealth.

Environments  - There are ultra-realistic harsh environments, dimly lit corridors, and hazardous areas included in the thirty levels set across three unique Maximum Security, Double-Max, and Triple-Max prison areas and more.
