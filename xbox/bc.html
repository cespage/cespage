Features

Gameplay  - Handpick tribe members to produce babies born to be hunters, mystics, fighters or other specialisations. As experience grows and time passes, choose the right skills and powers to enhance characters and benefit the tribe.

Combat  - Experience savage battles in an uncivilised, merciless era with brutal conflicts in a violent, dangerous world where only the strong survive.

Weapons  - Discover and create a multitude of deadly weapons using naturally occurring materials. Interactive environments allow for multiple solutions and any object can be picked up or used as a tool or weapon.

World  - Lead your tribe through pre-historic deadly but beautiful environments, from ancient rainforests to underwater settings, weather the elements from scathing desert heat to raging tropical rainstorms.
