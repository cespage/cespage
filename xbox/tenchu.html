Features

Gameplay  - Master combo attacks, including stealth moves and jumping attacks. Take on the role of three characters, Rikimaru, Ayame and Tesshu utilising over twenty authentic ninja weapons and tools plus earn new abilities and combos by racking up stealth kills.

Missions  - Complete unique mission objectives including espionage, theft and assassination. Levels allow you to determine the most efficient way to achieve mission goals with multiple game endings based on earlier decisions to keep you coming back for more.

Environment  - Features eleven detailed levels with realistic lighting and weather effects, breakable items and authentic character animations.

Xbox Live  - Xbox Live functionality features two-player co-op missions with all new stealth kill animations through six levels, as well as a two-player versus mode featuring twenty playable characters.
