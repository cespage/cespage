Tom Clancy's Splinter Cell is third person stealth based action game with cyber-terrorism, shadow agencies and covert operations. You are Sam Fisher, a field operative of a secretive agency. Features

Gameplay  - Third person persceptive stealth game where you can infiltrate high-security strongholds, neutralise anyone who opposes mission goals - from hackers to special forces soldiers, seize critical intelligence, destroy threatening data and equipment, and disappear without a trace.

Movement  - Animated movement created by hand rather than motion captured gives the movment a more artistic feel including climbing, zip-line, wall jumping, drop attack and more!

Environments  - True to life Dynamic lighting & shadowing, volumetric lighting, high resolution textures, glow effects, more varied and fluid sound effects and dialogues afforded by the hard drive.
