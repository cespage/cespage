Kameo is a magical, epic adventure full of magnificent exploration and intense combat. The future of the world depends on Kameo, who must capture, grow and unleash an assortment of incredible monsters to combat evil, and rescue her three Elemental Ancestors from the Dark Troll King, who threatens the planet and its wildlife.

Features

Gameplay  - Kameo has the unique ability to morph into a wide variety of incredible monsters, each with its own strengths and fighting style. To fight the hordes of evil you need to employ elemental monsters from the Elements of Power. Fast-paced action, epic-style battles and massive boss encounters will require you to think and act quickly.

Explore  - Monsters are not only key for combat, but they are vital tools to expand your journeys. Each monster offers unique abilities that can open up and safely explore new areas. Intelligent use of monsters is critical to solving puzzles and avoiding obstructions, traps and enemies.

Collect  - Kameo's powers grow through hunting and capturing a diverse collection of monsters, but this is no easy task. Join forces with sidekick Meepo and use stealth, quickness and puzzle-solving to capture these sneaky little creatures. They grow their monsters and expand their monster arsenal as they increase their ability to explore the world and defeat enemies and challenges.

Environment  - Explore a vast and magical world filled with lush environments, thriving wildlife, wondrous creatures, clever puzzles and big challenges. Environments with sprawling hills, fertile valleys and daunting lairs cover twenty fantasy-style locations. As your journey through the expansive land you can also take part in multiple amazing boss encounters.
