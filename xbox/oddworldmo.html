Oddworld: Munch's Oddysee allows you to embark on a journey through a changing world of oddball creatures, off-the-wall action and outlandish goons. Guide Mudokon champion Abe, and his aquatic Gabbit partner Munch in an adventure across Oddworld using special psychic powers, cool power-ups, way-out vehicles and other special abilities.

Features

Teamplay  - Use Munch and Abe co-operatively to make their way through the trials of Oddworld. Abe can climb and jump to reach new areas, then run to escape from dangerous enemies. When all else fails, chant to take control of the enemy's soul, and use their guns to clear a path to safety. Munch can swim around the new realms of Oddworld. When on the ground, zip along with a four-wheeled assist. Need to move some yapping Slogs out of the way? Just use Munch's nifty Sonar Plug to fire up the crane and drop those Sloggies in their pen.

Psychic  - Let Abe's chant roar to take possession of a gun-toting Big Bro Slig and clear almost anything out of the way. Munch can use his handy Sonar Plug to hijack nearby machines including cranes, security cameras and 50-caliber Anaesthesia bots to tackle the really tough obstacles.

Items  - Need to get somewhere quickly or get over a wall, like right now? Use Oddworld's consumer power-ups like Expresso and Klimb to amp up Munch and Abe's abilities to perform cool moves. For the more direct approach, snag high-calibre weaponry or pilfer cool vehicles to mow through tough situations and get around fast!

Environment  - Get Oddworld's inhabitants to carry out tricky tasks or just beat the bejesus out of enemies. Using the intelligent cinematic camera, auto-targeting system and no-stick navigation system makes playing a breeze!
