Features

Storyline  - In Advent Rising, a common legend pervades the galaxy-that of a powerful, highly intelligent ancient race that will one day deliver the universe. They are known simply as Humans. The Seekers, however, know that Humans truly do exist and see them as an enemy that must be destroyed. Under the guise of benevolent explorers they comb the universe and eradicate any Human colonies they find. Until now.

Control & Weapons  - Versatile control scheme that allows for acrobatic movement and precise targeting of enemies while quickly switching between weapons and powers. Wield an arsenal of awesome weapons, all with unique alternate fire abilities

Environment  - Unprecedented single-player game play allows gamers to play through the thrilling action sequences of a blockbuster movie or game, rather than just watch them. Players can choose to switch from 3rd to 1st person point-of-view.

Xbox Live  - Downloadable content from Xbox Live plus play online with your friends.
