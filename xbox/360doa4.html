Features

Gameplay  - Fighting system will be overhauled allowing even more mastery and strategy of your favourite character, including the most complex DOA countering system yet! Each character will have a variety of new costumes to rock while they fight, some characters will even sport new hair dos and this latest installment.

Stages & Characters  - Incredible new interactive stages showcasing the incredible 3-D graphics and astounding attention to detail from each strand of the character's hair to each cherry blossom falling from the sky. New characters added to the DOA all-star line-up, and the return of DOA3 favourites, Christie and Brad Wong!

Graphics  - Utilising the advanced technology of Xbox 360 and Team Ninja's development expertise, Dead or Alive 4 will redefine 3-D graphics creating the most visually stunning video game to date!

Xbox Live  - Unsurpassed online play via Xbox Live including large scale global online tournaments, increased worldwide simultaneous online play, detailed scoreboards, players can form clans, new and interactive lobby area featuring voice and text chat available, and more!
