Imagine being in Paradise for two whole weeks, with no-one but the lovely ladies of Dead or Alive to keep you company! Except instead of fighting - it's all about beach volleyball. You play as a character you select from the Dead or Alive ladies, enjoy holidays at various spots on the island playing beach volleyball games.

Features

Gameplay  - Accomplish activities on the island map as part of the game mission to win the beach volleyball games. Also compete in Main Mode and Versus Mode to win. The way you play earns you money you can spend on the island or gamble in the island's casino.

Intelligent  - The players aren't just good-looking - they hide some advanced AI which means every decision you make has an effect also be careful which character you pair with whom - there are old rivalries at play and you might find that co-operation is not the name of the game.

Personalise  - Personalise the characters in many ways, from choosing new outfits and nail colour to deciding whether or not they want their characters to have a suntan or not, but make your choices wisely - the characters might not like what you buy them and refuse to wear it.

Video & Audio  - The environments are beautifully rendered, with gameplay set in the morning, afternoon and evening - gamers will be amazed at the realistic lighting effects on this paradise island. Experience the incredible realism of the environmental sound effects and music bring the sounds of the seaside and an exciting sporting event to life.
