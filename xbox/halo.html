Features

Storyline  - Tranport yourself to the unknown ringworld of Halo and discover the many environments from tropical Islands to snow covered landscapes and fight the Covenant and more.

Gameplay  - Advance graphics system with realistic lighting effects and sounds, attach enemy outposts, rescue fallen conrads and more missions in a huge awe inspiring landscape.

Weapons & Vehicles  - Fight enemies with a vast array of human and alien weapons including pistols and the Covenant needler but choose carefully as you are limited to two weapons at a time. Take the wheel of the human Warthog or fly in the Covenant Banshee with a variety of human and alien weapons on board.

Multiplayer  - Fight it out in a variety of maps and locations on Halo including bases and places to hide - capture the flag, ride vehicles and battle your friends. Also take a friend with you on your adventure in Halo on the Coop mode!
