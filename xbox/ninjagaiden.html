Features

Gameplay  Your ninja skills will be put to the ultimate test as you battle through various levels in your mission to avenge your clan. Once you embark as a ninja hero you never have to stop the journey you can quit the game at any time and re-start at the very same point.

Weapons  - Through the progression of the game your skill will increase, also be rewarded with new weapons to fight your opponents with. As you become more competent fighting enemies, the stronger you become, unlocking new combinations and skills.

Environment  - Witness an amazing level of detail as you take on opponents in a variety of environments. Hear the swords slicing through the air, plus beautifully rendered graphics, amazing visual effects and realistic combat.

Xbox Live  - Compete in downloadable trial missions made available only through Xbox Live. Top-ranked players will win special prizes and be included in the Ninja Gaiden Hall of Fame.
