Features

The Incredibles  - All-new action/adventure game based on the world and characters from The Incredibles movie. Play as Mr. Incredible or Frozone at anytime.

Storyline  - The game begins exactly where the film ends and exclusively shows what occurred in the Incredibles' battle with the Underminer.

Co-op  - Cooperative game-play with the ability to switch characters at any point in the game.

Progress  - Update your character's moves and abilities as you progress through the game.
