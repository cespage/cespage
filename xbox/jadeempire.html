Features

Gameplay  - Jade Empire immerses you in a deep, rich storyline. A story that unfolds with drama, action, and adventure, other characters enrich the adventure by providing comic relief, rivalries, flirtations, and hostility.

Roleplay  - Choose a character with a unique appearance and quirks, a custom voice set, and a powerful combination of ability scores and fighting styles. Choose to act for good or evil, and rule with an iron fist or a velvet glove.

Fighting  - Jade Empire provides a gameplay experience with the tempo and fury. Upgrade or learn entirely new styles to tailor a character for specific gameplay preferences. Each fightinh style is a package of up to seven moves, each with their own speed, power, and form, that range from punches and kicks to weapon strikes, mystical powers.

World  - Attract followers, and send them on missions across the Jade Empire. Take over a fortified town and turn a base of operations, visit it to interact with followers, send agents out on missions, learn new fighting styles, and attract and train students.
