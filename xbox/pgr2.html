Features

Gameplay  - Project Gotham Racing 2 is the sequel to Project Gotham Racing. This game rewards drivers for focusing on speed and style while still taking risks around every corner, Project Gotham Racing 2 offers circuit-based racing through highly detailed, photorealistic urban environments. Choose from more than 50 of the hottest cars while learning your way around new and exciting cities. Race against up to three friends at once through a multiplayer SystemLink or match up against players online utilising Xbox Live.

Kudos  - Gamers can experience more ways to earn Kudos, as well as a new rating system with a more accurate way to rank players rather than just by the number of Kudos they have earned.

Circuits  - Maintaining its international theme, the game allows players to travel the globe racing in some of the world's most dynamic cities. Cities include Hong Kong, Edinburgh, Barcelona and Paris (via Content Download). Also race in a wide range of high-performance vehicles, including models from top manufacturers such as the Enzo Ferrari and Porsche 911.

Xbox Live  - Project Gotham Racing 2 supports Xbox Live with online play, allowing gamers to challenge friends and others with ghost, weekly and monthly challenges plus players will also be able to get downloadable content such as expansion parks like the Paris download.
