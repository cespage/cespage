Enter the Classic World of James Bond. The recipe is this: take three measures of stealth, driving, and jetpack flying and mix them into missions of great intrigue. Add in dangerously beautiful women and garnish with one 007 played by the original James Bond - Sean Connery. Serve in an authentic and immersive re-creation of the classic 007 world and you have From Russia with Love, an interactive experience that drops you deep into the plot of the legendary film...with a few surprises in store that will leave you shaken and stirred.
