Features

Gameplay  - From broken windows to missing hoods, this game features an updated graphics engine with realistic vehicle physics complete with damage modelling and performance degradation to test a driver's mettle.

Cars  - Features more than fourty of the most challenging rally cars and also make minor modifications to each car to meet the challenge of each track.

Weather  - Race on different tracks from Australia, the frozen Nordic and the Pacific Northwest and variable weather conditions including rain, snow and fog.Test every driving skill you think you have and some you don't even know were physically possible. Interactive environments including breakaway signs, moving rocks, and new splash and rain effects.

Xbox Live  - Take out your rival over Xbox Live, and track up-to-date stats throughout your own rally season. Compete in your own virtual sports league with XSN Sports.
