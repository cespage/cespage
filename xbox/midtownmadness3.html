Features

Gameplay  - Three different race modes such as Blitz, Checkpoint and Cruise. You can also just drive through the incredibly vast cityscapes of DC or Paris, or race through and unlock more than 40 different Blitz and Checkpoint races.

Vehicles  - Have the opportunity to put their driving skills to the test across a wide assortment of fast and fun vehicles. Select from more than 30 vehicles, including licensed cars like the BMW Mini Cooper, the Lotus Espirit Turbo, or the Hummer HS as well as vehicles such as city buses, taxis or even garbage trucks.

Environment  - Highly detailed, fully researched renditions of Paris and Washington, complete with ambient traffic and animated pedestrians. There are also hidden routes, shortcuts and hideouts are encountered throughout each of the cities, showcasing the incredible depth of these environments.

Xbox Live  - Challenge friends and others with online play via Xbox Live. Xbox Live includes downloadable content and support up to eight players with four game modes which are Capture the Gold, Stay away, Tag and Hunter.
