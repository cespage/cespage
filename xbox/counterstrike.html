Features

Gameplay  - Experience a deep, involving counter-terrorism world in which danger abounds. Lead an elite team through the jungles of Asia and the cold regions of Eastern Europe, and in the unbearable humidity of Colombia.

Counter-Terrorism  - Engage in counter-terrorist activities such as Bomb Defuse and Hostage Rescue and also play the new single-player component as well as the graphically enhanced missions from Counter-Strike: Condition Zero.

Weapons & Gadgets  - Choose from more than 25 real-life weapons including Shotguns, sniper rifles, pistols and other military artillery are available. Gadgets include a riot shield, blowtorch and fibre-optic camera.

Xbox Live  - Battle other players throughout the world with Xbox Live. A unique player-ranking system will allow gamers to select games with equal-calibre opponents, while Xbox Live helps ensure cheat-free playing fields. Also download additional content from Xbox Live.
