Features

Control  - Use the right analogue stick to find open receivers heading for paydirt. Use the left analogue stick to lead a receiver to an open area, throw away from a defender, and more.

NFL Superstar Mode  - Create a Superstar and guide his life on and off the field. Choose an agent, earn one of 60 personas, and live the superstar life.

Vision and Precision  - Get a detailed analysis of the new Vision Cone and Precision Passing systems and find out what top NFL Quarterbacks think of the features. We break down the new feature and detail what it takes to build your dream NFL player.

Enhanced Broadcast Presentation  - Authentic new camera angles, improved graphics, and new commentary and cutscenes that provide the look and feel of a real NFL broadcast.
