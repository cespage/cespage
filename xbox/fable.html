Fable is a ground-breaking role-playing adventure game from Peter Molyneux, in which your every action determines your skills, appearance, and reputation. The character's life story is created from childhood to death and can grow from an inexperienced child into the most powerful being in the world. Choose the path of righteousness, or dedicate their life to evil.

Features

Gameplay  - Heroes age and evolve through the actions you choose and the paths followed - be it for good, evil or in between. Those who ply the way of the sword will see their muscles bulge. Those who weave the dark arts will witness power crackle at their fingertips. And those who skulk in the shadows will watch their skin bleach.

Combat  - Collect battle scars as you duel with cunning foes and deadly creatures. Master an array of deadly weapons and hone the art of blade-craft, hunt quarry using subterfuge and stealth, or weave death from the elements as you harness the dark arts of the arcane.

Skills  - Champion or manipulate an ever-changing land with competitive and cooperative heroes, dynamic weather systems, and deformable environments teeming with cultures, creatures, citizens and cities.

World  - Explore six different exotic worlds, each with a unique environment, enemies and strategies. Learn how to best use the unique abilities of the commandos to survive in each world.
