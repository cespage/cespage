Features

Xbox Live Communicator  - This is your headset and microphone that allows you to communicate while playing games on Xbox Live.

Xbox Live Starter Disc  - You will need to run this disc on your Xbox in order to update your Xbox Dashboard for Live features and gameplay.

12 Month Xbox Live Subscription  - Enter the code for 12 months of Xbox Live service during the account setup process, the subscription starts from the day the account is setup.

Live-enabled Games  - Xbox Live supports online play to get you into Xbox Live two demo games of Moto GP and Whacked are included free!
