R-Type Dimensions combines the genre-defining coin-op classics R-Type and R-Type II, with a twist. Although classic gameplay remains untouched, R-Type Dimensions elevates the experience to greater heights with brand-new additions and enhancements.

Features

Gameplay  - Fight through all 14 Bydo-infested stages in the original 2-D or all-new 3-D graphics with the ability to swap back and forth between the two seamlessly. Strike down the evil Bydo Empire and save the earth in R-Type Dimensions.

Gamemodes  - Two full playable classic coin-op games R-Type and R-Type II with Classic game mode, play that exactly mimics the original coin-op experiences.

Skill  All-new Infinite game mode assuring completion of games regardless of skill level plus Statistics tracking track all the player's vital stats.

Xbox Live  Play Locally or on Xbox LIVE for up to two players. Two free gamer pictures can be unlocked by players of all skill levels. Achievements range from easy to advanced, making them appropriate for every skill level.
