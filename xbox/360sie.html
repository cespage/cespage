Features

Gameplay  - SPACE INVADERS gets a makeover! Revamped graphics, new visualisers exclusive to the Xbox 360 created by Jeff Minter (Llamasoft), as well as upbeat electronic tracks perfectly synched to gameplay.

Twists  - Features, Breaks and Fever Times, Discover exciting new twists to the classic game that provide exciting, fast-paced and challenging gameplay.

Mini-Game  Experience varied gameplay and rack up points with 16 different types of the "Round" mini-games launched mid-game.

Xbox Live  - Battle against or with friends locally or compete online for the highest leaderboard scores. VS Mode : Last man standing wins in Survivor Mode; player with most point wins in Score Attack Mode. Co-op Mode : Up to four players tackle the Arcade Mode; take down the toughest invaders with more firepower as you share a single pool of lives.
