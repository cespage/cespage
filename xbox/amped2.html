Features

Gameplay  - Snowboarders can attack the mountain, do trick or spin to earn big points. Or getting big points tricks with "steez," like tweaks, rotations or rail balancing.

New tricks  - Amped 2 takes the moves from the first and adds new aerial tricks, advanced grabs, rail slides, tweaks, stalls and more. There is a tutorial added to teach first-timers the basics of freestyle riding.

Xbox Live  - With a worldwide nation of snowboarders via Xbox Live, gamers will be able to challenge riders around the globe to see who's the best. Check the stats for regional and worldwide ladders to see rankings. Trade replays with friends or download new content including mountains, gear, challenges and music.
