Unlike any console before, Xbox 360 puts gamers at the center and gives you the freedom to personalise your gaming experience however you see fit. Xbox 360 Faces make it easy and fun to change the face of your console. Change your Faces whenever you like to match your personality, your mood, or your wild side. So flip it, switch it, or leave it be, and express your unique sense of style.

Features

Customise  - Interchangeable faceplates allow you to express yourself and create a one-of-a-kind system. Find cool, customisable, cutting-edge designs that are fun to change.

Designs  - Faces are highly versatile, with multiple designs to choose from. Faces are designed for easy switching.

Choice  - Maintain full functionality of your system, regardless of which Face you choose. Pick up copies of the collectable limited-edition Faces.

Skins  - Download corresponding skins from the Xbox Live Marketplace and customise the Xbox Gamer Guide interface to match your faceplate.
