Features

Gameplay  - Choose how to approach missions and eliminate the targets in hundreds of ways, there are no rules just get the target.

Hitman  - Become the world's best Hitman in twelve different levels, with multiple contracts in each one.

Weapons  - Over 60 different weapons can be discovered throughout the game, including some very innovative but some are brutal close combat weapons.

Environment  - New, integrated technology, including new particle effects, new reflective surface effects and new physics enhances the environments and the suspense.
