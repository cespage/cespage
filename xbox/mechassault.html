MechAssault lets you pilot the most powerful weapon available in the 31st century, a 40-foot-tall walking tank known as a BattleMech, or Mech. Choose from a array of powerful Mechs - ranging from quick 30-tonners to huge 100-ton beasts - which you can upgrade and optimise mid-mission to crush their opponents.

Features

Gameplay  - You are given their military objectives by operatives on the ground and must figure out the best way to accomplish them. Hit the ground running and enjoy the unexpected events turn routine missions into chaotic all-out frag fests.

Mechs  - To pilot the most awesome piece of military machinery ever created, you can choose from powerful, unique and formidable BattleMechs, each with enough firepower to level a city - literally. Mid-mission weapon upgrades allow you to customise the 'Mech chassis to suit your style or mood. Swap out weapons, outfit the 'Mech with Jump Jets or maximise armor - the choice is yours.

Environment  - Every structure, bridge, tower and bus is fully destructible. Blow away everything in the world bit by bit, blasting the side of a building exposes rebar, hanging insulation and even enemy snipers! Even can use the environment against opponents. If you're running low on ammo, drop a 20-story building on the infantry and save the firepower for the big guys.

Xbox Live  - Battle friends or enemies over the Internet using the multiplayer feature with Xbox Live, taunt the competition or chat with a teammates using full voice communication.
