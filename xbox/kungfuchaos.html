Kung Fu Chaos is a 70's-style kung fu brawler with a zany cast of characters who battle one another on parodied movie sets. Be immersed in the kung fu element as you perform over-the-top gigantic leaps, suicidal stunts and multiple-opponent action all while unleashing humiliating insults that can be more damaging than karate chops or kicks.

Features

Gameplay  - Featuring a wide range of single- and multiplayer gameplay modes, mini games, team-play possibilities, customisable options and unlockable features, Kung Fu Chaos is a pick-up-and-play gameplay extravaganza designed for a wide range of players.

Combat  - It will take more than just a cool move to win this game, Kung Fu Chaos features an in-depth combat system with the added twist of humiliation. With signature moves, multiple combo attacks, blocks, counter attacks and interrupters, you can pummel opponents with slick moves or power up with taunts that add to the impact. Combat moves are easy to learn but deep enough for experienced players to gain new skills and really go to town on their enemies.

Characters  - Kung Fu Chaos features an outrageous cast of would-be superstars, each character has a unique fighting style, an array of hilarious mistranslated taunts and a repertoire of spectacular special attacks.

Environment  - Huge animating structures, traveling sets, incidental details and spectacular action sequences all come together to deliver epic battles that are fast-paced, highly competitive and downright funny.
