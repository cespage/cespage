Features

Control  - The integrated lap mount lets you steer your virtual racecar from anywhere in the room and looking for a tighter racing feel? Utilise the included table mount for extra stability. Designed for superior slip resistance, the tethered foot pedals use your heel to stay in place and feature tension and resistance for an unrivaled sense of realism.

Force Feedback  - Force Feedback and Rumble replicate the yanks and shudders that a racecar driver feels while blasting around the oval. Just plug in the AC adapter to activate Force Feedback. If you prefer the freedom of wireless gaming, rely on the wheel's powerful dual Rumble motors instead.

Wireless Technlogy  - Drive with confidence and zero loss of performance knowing that the wheel is powered by the same award-winning wireless technology used in the Xbox 360 Wireless Controller.

Customise  - When you're ready to take your gaming to the next level, go online with Xbox Live to join millions of Xbox live members. Play with and against the best online racers on the planet. Or just chat, text, and watch them speed their way to the finish line. To keep your controller race ready and fully charged, consider purchasing the Xbox 360 Quick Charge Kit. To connect with teammates or opponents on Xbox Live, consider purchasing the Xbox 360 Wireless Headset.
