Street Fighter II defined the 1990s gaming era and revolutionized the fighting game genre. Regarded as one of the greatest games of all time, Street Fighter II' Hyper Fighting was the third and final release of the original Street Fighter II series. Featuring the original eight world warriors including Ryu, Chun Li, and Guile - along with playable boss characters Balrog, Sagat, and M. Bison - Street Fighter II'Hyper Fighting offered enhanced gameplay speed and improved character balancing.

Features

Gameplay  - Arcade Mode simulates the single-player Street Fighter experience where gamers must work their way through a ladder of opponents until they face off against the final boss and Vs mode allows two friends to select characters and battle it out.

Features  - Players can map attacks to any of the main buttons and have the choice to map all three punches and/or all three kicks to a single button which simplifies complex moves

Leaderboards  - Tracks overall and monthly best players using a point based system as well as rankings based on who is the best using each game character and most consecutive winning streaks

Xbox Live  - Jump into a match quickly for instant action, the best possible match is determined by sorting through a set of filters including number of rounds, turbo speed, chat on or off and friends only. Create Game is the most popular method for coordinating invite-only matches among friends which allows you to choose among several settings and host games. Quarter Match is an entirely new mode for Xbox Live Arcade which is a spectator-based system as a tribute to the arcade roots and allows online competitors to use a "virtual quarter" to "buy into" heated two player matches.
