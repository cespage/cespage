The Xbox 360 Headset heightens the experience of the unprecedented online gaming community, Xbox Live, allowing you to strategise with teammates, or just chat with friends while playing your favourite games. You can record and leave short voice messages for friends or family through the free Xbox Live Message Service or connect with buddies online using the headset in place of the phone.

Features

Quality  - Boom microphone can be adjusted for optimal sound quality. Lightweight, over-the-head design enables comfortable use.

Sound  - In-line volume control and mute switch let users adjust the headset volume or switch mute on and off easily.

Communicate  - Noise-canceling microphone improves game response to voice commands, and provides crisp and clear voice communication.

Connect  - The headset plugs directly into either the Xbox 360 Controller or Xbox Wireless Controller for easy use.
