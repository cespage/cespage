Features

Graphics  - Xbox sets a new standard in video game graphics. The graphics processor allows artists to design games with amazing realism, and many of these games only appear on Xbox.

Sound  - Just like watching a movie in surround sound, experience video game audio like never before and feel like you are actually in the game.

Storage  - The built in hard drive allows you to explore bigger and more detailed fantasy worlds without interruption from loading screens. Customise the music and listen to them while playing games. Save your games and start from the same place you left tpreviously. Download additional content for your games, like new characters and extra levels, and save them on your console from Xbox Live.

Xbox Live  - Xbox was designed from the start to be the future of on-line gaming: Online team play, new levels, new characters will all be waiting for you. Head-to-head team play with friends across the street or across the world.
