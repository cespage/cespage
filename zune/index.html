Zune HD available to Preorder

Zune HD is the next generation of Microsoft's portable digital media player, providing easy access to all your favorite digital entertainment. It is the first portable media player to combine a built-in HD Radio receiver, HD video output capabilities , OLED touch screen, Wi-Fi and an Internet browser.
Zune HD has a sleek and stylish design and is available in Black 16GB or Platinum 32GB. Both capacities of Zune HD will be available for pre-order through major retailers such as Amazon, Best Buy, Walmart and Microsoft.
