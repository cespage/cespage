Features

Your Music. Your Choice - Choose from a selection of more than 1 million DRM-free MP3s. Download to play on your Zune device or any other MP3 player.

Music videos and podcasts - In addition to a wide selection of music, Zune Marketplace offers over 1,500 music videos and more than 1,000 featured audio and video podcasts. You can also add your own podcasts by simply typing in the URL.

Custom playlists - Zune Marketplace offers more than 1,200 hand-picked playlists from Marketplace editors and special guests, with new playlists available for download weekly. You can also express yourself by submitting your own playlist.

Pay-per-song? or download at a flat rate? - Downloading songs and music videos a la carte from Zune Marketplace is simple using Microsoft Points, which work across Zune and Xbox LIVE Marketplace. Or choose Zune Pass, which gives you unlimited access to millions of available songs for a flat rate of $14.99 (U.S.) per month. Fill your device with new music whenever you want and keep those songs as long as you keep your Zune Pass up to date.
