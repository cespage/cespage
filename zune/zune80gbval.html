Features

Wireless - Cut the cord to your PC. Refresh your Zune with new content anywhere you charge your device. Connect to your PC through your home wireless network by setting your Zune to automatically sync the latest podcasts, music and more from your stereo dock, AC adapter or speaker dock accessory.
Share full-length tracks of your favorite songs, albums, playlists, pictures and even audio podcasts. Listen to the full track of any song you receive up to three times, and even pass along songs you receive to other friends who have a Zune. If you like a song you receive, you can easily add it to your wish list and then buy it from your PC the next time you sync.

Connect - The Zune software will now automatically import broadcast content recorded on Windows Media Center for Windows Vista, With the built-in FM tuner you can listen to your favorite local FM radio stations while you're on the go.
Plug your Zune into your Xbox 360 and customize the soundtrack in your favourite games. You can also stream the music, pictures and videos from the Zune software on your PC to your Xbox 360 console using a wireless connection.

Player - A bright, durable 8.1cm glass screen delivers uncompromised portable video viewing. Your music, video and pictures never looked better. Fly effortlessly through your song, picture and video collections with the new Zune Pad with innovative new touch controls let you brush or click.

Community & Marketplace - Discover, share and leave your musical mark. Create a Zune Card and personal profile at Zune.net to reflect your musical identity. Invite friends, share song recommendations, post comments and browse member pages to discover new music and connect to other people with similar musical tastes. Get your fill at the Marketplace. Over 3 million songs, albums, DRM-free MP3s, music videos, audio and video podcasts and more. Buy individual songs and albums or choose a Zune Pass to get unlimited downloads for a flat rate.

Specifications

Colour

  • Red

Navigation

  • Zune Pad touch controls

Capacity

  • 80 GB (20,000 songs or 25,000 pictures or 250 hours of video)

Audio

  • AAC (up to 320 Kbps)
  • MP3 (up to 320 Kbps)
  • WMA (up to 384 Kbps)
  • Protected WMA (Zune Marketplace)
  • WMA Lossless

Video

  • WMV (720x480)
  • h.264 (720x480)
  • MPEG-4 (720x480)
  • DVR-MS (will transcode at the time of sync)

Pictures

  • JPEG

Display

  • Size: 3.2 inches / 8.1cm
  • Orientation: Vertical and Horizontal
  • Resolution: 320 x 240 pixels

Wireless

  • Wireless sync, audio/picture send/receive
  • Connectivity: 802.11 b/g
  • Range: Up to 30 feet

Battery

  • Music - Up to 30 hours (wireless off).
  • Video - Up to 4 hours.
  • Charge - 2 hours (1 hour quick charge to 90% capacity)

Size and Weight

  • 61.1W x 108.2H x 12.9D mm
  • Weight: 4.5 ounces / 128g
