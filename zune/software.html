Features

Manage your collection - In the Zune software's streamlined new design, your music, pictures, videos and podcasts take centre stage. Ripping and burning CDs, creating playlists, and syncing new content to your device have never been easier.

Import your music - Zune software will automatically import your existing music, pictures and videos, including music you have ripped into iTunes and Microsoft Windows Media Player, even transferring your existing song ratings in one easy step. Some content may be protected by the service you purchased it from, so those files cannot be imported.

Video to go - Zune will automatically import unprotected .WMV, .MPG4 and H.264 files you have created yourself or downloaded from the Internet. If you're using Windows Vista Home Premium or Ultimate, you can also import TV shows or movies recorded using Windows Media Center on your PC to sync to your device. Load up your favourite videos and hit the road.

Your games. Your music. Your way. - Plug your Zune into your Xbox 360 console to customize the soundtrack in supported games. You can also stream the music, pictures and videos from the Zune software on your PC to your Xbox 360 console using a wireless connection.
