ZuneCardr

It has been a busy week a new software product from Comentsys, ZuneCardr launched officially today this adds new functionality and features to the software that allows image-based Zune Cards to be created in a variety of styles and themes.
This means that this week's update is a bit shorter than normal, given the time to launch and test this new software, but back to normal next week, plus as a bonus there will be an exclusive article on the creation of this software with some code posted and insight into the design and development of this application. Also this week there will be no tutorial however next week there will be two, along with this article and possibly a new video tutorial so hopefully that will make up for this week's lack of update!
