Home of CESNote.com the Desktop Notes application with Compact, Standard and Premiere Editions.

Editions

CESNote.com Compact

Desktop, Doodle, Task and Photo Notes plus Notes management, customisation Options and more. The Compact Desktop Notes experience.

CESNote.com Standard

All previous notes plus Web, Event, Contact and Launch notes, plus Notes Management, Pinboard, Options, Themes and more. The new Standard for Desktop Notes.

CESNote.com Premiere

All previous notes plus NexusNote, Feed, KlikType and Audio Note, plus Notes Management, Pinboard, Options, Themes, Online features and more. The Premiere Desktop Notes experience.
