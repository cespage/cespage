HALO WAYPOINT

Halo Waypoint is the destination on Windows Phone 7 for all things Halo, including Videos, Exclusive content, Forums, Podcasts and more.

features

Career  Xbox LIVE® Members can more form Halo Waypoint by viewing their Halo Career information including information about achievements earned and career score for games such as Halo 3, Halo ODST, Halo Wars and Halo Reach.

Video  View trailers and exclusive content from Halo Waypoint including Halo Reach live action trailers to premium content like Red vs Blue from Rooster Teeth, Halo Legends and other content inspired from the Halo Universe.

Community  Link to the Forums and chat and discuss all things halo, plus listen to podcasts on Halo including the Official Bungie podcast and get insight into the development of projects such as Halo Reach, or discuss you favourite maps and gameplay with other players or view Tweets about Halo from Twitter.
