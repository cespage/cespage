ZUNECARDR

ZuneCardr allows you to view Zune® Cards of Zune Social Members, such as their favourite tracks, recently played tracks and artists, badges and social information. Plus even when you are not connected you can still view all the zune cards you have downloaded and refresh them when you want to see their latest Zune Card!

features

Zune Card  View Zune Card information including Zune Tag, Recent Played Tracks, Played Artists, Badges Earned, Total Plays, Zune Card Tile and Background of any Zune® social member whenever you want.

More Music  Zune Card information can show you the recently played tracks of any Zune Social member including their favourites, plus you can tap on any track / album art to see links to more information such as information on Zune social and where you can purchase the track.

More Artists  You can view a Zune Social member's artists they have listed to, either from the Track information or the Played Artists section and also link to that artist's page on Zune Social, where even more information about that artist can be found.

More Social  See what Badges a Zune Social member has earned from the music they have listened to or their participation in the wider Zune community, also you can view their profile infomation with their total plays, name and biography information - find like minded people you like the music you like or find a new friend to share your music with.
