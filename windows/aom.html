Features

Gameplay - An epic singleplayer campaign, singleplayer games against challenging AI opponents, more than 15 different random map types, nine highly differentiated civilizations.

Mythology - In their struggle for supremacy, players can use mythological creatures like Minotaurs and Cyclopses to bolster their armies' strength. Players may also call upon the gods for assistance in flattening enemy towns with meteors or scattering opposing troops with lightning storms.

Environment - Lifelike terrain, beautiful waves and water, realistic sunlight, and lush forests. The units roaming are incredibly detailed and move naturally over the terrain. Amazing special effects when the gods get involved, all grows dark as an eclipse takes effect and funnel clouds twist and grow as a tornado sends buildings spinning toward the heavens.

Online - Easy-to-use multiplayer gaming online, play your friends with Greek vs Norse and other variations use the God Powers to strike or build a massive army and attack their settlements.
