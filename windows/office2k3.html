Features

Suite - Microsoft Office 2003 is a suite of applications including Access for databases, Excel for speadsheets and accounts, OneNote for general notes, Powerpoint for presentations, Publisher for desktop publishing and Word for word processing.

Additional - Not just restricted to these applications others are available a small free application called Picture Manager is inlcuded but others such as Frontpage for website design, Infopath for Forms and XML, Live Meeting for online meetings, Project for project management and Visio for application and general planning/design.

Features - All the applications have many great features including the Tablet PC compatibility of OneNote allowing written notes. WordArt in Word, create eye catching titles and documents.

Online - Office 2003 has online integration share items using the Microsoft SharePoint facility (available as an online service or seperate product), plus download templates and more on Office Online.
